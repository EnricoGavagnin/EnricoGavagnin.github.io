SOCIAL ANT IMMUNOLOGY

From collective behaviour to social immunity

In March 2020 I will be joining the Stroemeyt Lab (https://stroeymeyt-lab.ch) as Research Associate at the Department of Life Sciences of the University of Bristol. I will try to understand how ants control epidemic outbreaks by adapting their colony social network. I will combine experimental data obtained by using automated tracking systems and social network analysis.
